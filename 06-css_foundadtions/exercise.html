From Garden to Plate

A potager is a French term for an ornament vegetable or kitchen garden. Often flowers edible and non-edible and herbs are planted with the vegetables to enhance the gardens beauty.The goal is to make the function of provideing food aesthetically pleasing.

What to Plant

Plants are chosen as much for thier functionality as for thier color and form. Many are trained to grow upward. A well-designed potager can provide food, cut flowers ang herbs for the home with very little maintenance. Potagers can disguise thier function of providing for a home in a wide array of forms - from the carefree style of the cottage garden to the formality of a knot garden.
